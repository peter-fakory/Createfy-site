Is this service for you?

E-commerce support is a good fit if you:

  • Are ready to move beyond taking orders manually via DMs or email.
  • Want a proper online store that feels trustworthy to customers.
  • Need someone to handle the setup and then teach you how to run it.
  • Are not sure which platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.) is right for you.

How we build your store

Our process keeps you involved without overwhelming you:

  1. 1. Discovery. We learn what you sell, how you deliver, and how you currently take orders.
  2. 2. Platform & plan. We recommend a platform and outline a simple build plan.
  3. 3. Build & populate. We configure the store, add products, and set up key pages.
  4. 4. Training & launch. We show you how to manage your store and support you through launch.
