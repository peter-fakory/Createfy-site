Our Story

Founded in Calgary in 2015, Createfy was born from a simple observation: too many great businesses were being held back by expensive, complicated, and outdated web solutions.

We saw talented entrepreneurs struggling with DIY website builders, paying thousands upfront for mediocre results, or worse—having no online presence at all.

So we set out to change that. We created a subscription model that makes premium web design accessible, affordable, and stress-free for businesses of all sizes.

Today, we're proud to serve over 200 Canadian businesses, from Calgary corner cafes to coast-to-coast e-commerce stores.
