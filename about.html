About

Our Story & Beliefs

We exist to glorify God by making disciples who love God, love people, and serve the world. Founded in 1998, our church has grown into a diverse family united by the gospel.

What We Believe

  • The Bible is God's inspired Word.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The Church is the body of Christ, called to mission.

Leadership

Our pastors, elders, and staff serve together to shepherd the church.
