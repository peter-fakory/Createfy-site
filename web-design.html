Web design packages

Every project is a little different, so pricing is custom. These packages give you a sense of how we usually work together. On our call, we’ll match you to the right option based on your goals, timeline, and budget.

Starter Website

For solo professionals and very small businesses getting online for the first time.

  • Clean, modern 1–3 page site (e.g., Home, About, Contact).
  • Mobile-friendly design that looks good on phones and laptops.
  • Simple contact form so people can reach you easily.
  • Basic on-page SEO (titles, descriptions, headings).
  • Launch support and basic “how to update” walkthrough.

Great if you just need a professional presence that you’re not embarrassed to share.

Business Website

For growing businesses that need to explain their services and capture more leads.

  • Multi-page site (typically 5–8 pages: services, portfolio, FAQs, etc.).
  • Clear calls-to-action to drive calls, bookings, or quote requests.
  • Service pages written to explain what you do in plain language.
  • Basic analytics setup so you can see who’s visiting and from where.
  • Integration with your preferred tools where possible (email, booking, etc.).

Ideal if you rely on your website to answer questions, build trust, and bring in steady enquiries.

How working with Createfy works

We keep the process simple and transparent from day one:

  1. 1. Free consult. We hop on a short call or exchange emails to understand your business, your goals, and what “good” looks like to you.
  2. 2. Proposal & scope. You’ll get a clear breakdown of what we’ll build, timelines, and a custom price based on the package that fits you best.
  3. 3. Design & build. We create your site, share previews, gather your feedback, and make adjustments until you’re happy.
  4. 4. Launch & handover. We launch your site, do final checks, and walk you through how to make simple updates yourself.
